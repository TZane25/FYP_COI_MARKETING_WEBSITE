WHY USE GO RPA

Efficiency

It can process repeated task you are working on automatically. What's more, you could be working on something else while the system could automate the repeated task for you. It saves time and energy.

Cost Management

Reduces unnecessary costs such as labour costs due to the decrease in the use of time due to the increase in productivity.

Reduce Risks

As there are no humans entering data in-between software, there would be lesser chances of wrongly inputted data. Hence, eliminating human errors.

WHY USE GO BWA

Uses Microsoft 365

Many companies everywhere are using Microsoft these days in their daily work environment. So goBWA helps to train you effectively in using Microsoft 365 applications to capture, process, and present data in a auto-efficient way.

Being Organised

Customers are allocated uniquely when they fill up the forms, so that when data is extracted into the excel sheet or a database, it would be sorted out automatically and accordingly.

Generate Emails

Tired of issuing emails to every customers manually? goBWA allows you to create an automated email response to customers whenever they had fill up a form or a feedback.
